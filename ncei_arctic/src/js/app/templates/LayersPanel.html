NOAA's National Centers for Environmental Information (NCEI) provides public access to a significant collection of weather, climate, coastal, oceanographic, and geophysical data. The Arctic Data Viewer, developed by NCEI's Arctic Action Team, highlights NCEI data and products that play a critical role in supporting scientific research, ecosystem health, community resilience, vibrant economies, and emergency response efforts in the region. This proof-of-concept interactive map viewer allows representative data sets from the Center for Coasts, Oceans, and Geophysics (CCOG), and the Climate Weather Center (CWC) archives to be displayed simultaneously, enabling exploration of spatial relationships between the observations, the natural environment, and human activities. The Team is continuing the effort to make NCEI's Arctic data holdings available via web services, thereby increasing their potential discovery and usability in GIS applications.
