The National Geophysical Data Center is the official distribution point for NOS bathymetric maps, bathymetric/fishing maps, regional maps, geophysical maps, and preliminary maps.

For more information, please visit: http://ngdc.noaa.gov/mgg/bathymetry/maps/nos_intro.html.

Basemap/reference layers:

  • Terrain (GEBCO_08): The GEBCO_08 Grid
  • Terrain (ETOPO1): ETOPO1 Global Relief Model
  • Simple background: Esri Light Gray Canvas
  • Imagery: Esri World Imagery
  • Ocean: Esri Ocean Basemap
  • National Geographic: Esri/National Geographic Basemap
  • Boundaries/Labels: World Boundaries and Places (Esri)

Map projection:

  • Global view: WGS84 Web Mercator (Auxiliary Sphere); EPSG:3857

Disclaimer:

  • All international boundaries, maritime boundaries, and placenames are shown for general orientation and reference only; they do not necessarily represent official U.S. policy.
  • WARNING: These data not to be used for navigation. Although these data are of high quality and useful for planning and modeling purposes, they are not suitable for navigation.
