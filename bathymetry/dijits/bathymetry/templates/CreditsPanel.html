For more information about the data layers included in this map:

  • Multibeam Bathymetry Surveys
  • Trackline Bathymetry Surveys
  • NOS Hydrographic Surveys
  • Digital Elevation Models (and shaded-relief imagery)

Basemap/reference layers:

  • Terrain (GEBCO_08): The GEBCO_08 Grid
  • Terrain (ETOPO1): ETOPO1 Global Relief Model
  • Simple background: Esri Light Gray Canvas
  • Simple background (Arctic view): GSHHS version 2.0 (Global Self-consistent, Hierarchical, High-resolution Shoreline Database)
  • Shaded Relief (Arctic view): Composite of IBCAO version 2.0 and GEBCO_08.
  • Imagery: Esri World Imagery
  • Ocean: Esri Ocean Basemap
  • National Geographic: Esri/National Geographic Basemap
  • Boundaries/Labels (Global view): World Boundaries and Places (Esri)
  • Boundaries/Labels (Arctic view):
    • World Maritime Boundaries: VLIZ Maritime Boundaries Geodatabase (version 5.0, 2009)
    • U.S. Exclusive Economic Zone: NOAA Office of Coast Survey
    • World City Locations: ESRI Data and Maps DVDs
    • Geographic/marine region labels, country boundaries, state/province boundaries, rivers, lakes: Natural Earth Data

Map projections:

  • Global view: WGS84 Web Mercator (Auxiliary Sphere); EPSG:3857
  • Arctic view: WGS84 Lambert Azimuthal Equal Area; central meridian of -150 degrees; EPSG:3572
