Search Surveys: specify a desired range of survey years, platform name, source institution, and survey ID. After clicking "OK", a filter will be applied to the Multibeam, Single-Beam, and NOS Hydrographic Surveys layers, displaying only data of interest. The current filter is displayed below the "Search Surveys" button. Note: only the survey ID filter applies to the BAG Footprints/Hillshades. The filter does not apply to DEMs or OCM Bathymetric Lidar.
Reset: clears the current filter.
