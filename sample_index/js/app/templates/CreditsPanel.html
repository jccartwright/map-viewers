What is the Index to Marine and Lacustrine Geological Samples?

The Index to Marine and Lacustrine Geological Samples is a tool to help scientists locate and obtain geologic material from sea floor and lakebed cores, grabs, and dredges archived by participating institutions. Before proposing research on any sample, please contact the curator for sample condition and availability.

The Index includes basic collection and storage information. Descriptions and additional information are available for some samples, as well as links to view and download related data and images at participating institutions and NGDC. An alternative text interface is available. View metadata

Data Sources and Credits:

Data are contributed by the sample repositories. Several repositories receive funding from the National Science Foundation (NSF) to prepare data for the Index. NGDC maintains the Index on behalf of the participating institutions. For more information, please see http://www.ngdc.noaa.gov/mgg/curator/
