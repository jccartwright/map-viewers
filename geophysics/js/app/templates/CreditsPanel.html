Map projections:

  • Global view: WGS84 Web Mercator (Auxiliary Sphere); EPSG:3857
  • Arctic view: WGS84 Arctic Polar Sterographic; EPSG:3995
  • Antarctic view: WGS84 Antarctic Polar Sterographic; EPSG:3031
