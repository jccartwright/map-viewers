• Recreational Facilities
    Marinas and Boat Ramps (US Only)

    The Gulf of Mexico is a playground for both locals and visitors who enjoy recreational activities on the water, such as boating, fishing, water skiing, and scuba diving. Marinas are located all across the coastal Gulf states, providing boat slips, fuel, food, bait, and more, to local boat owners, transient boaters, and charters. Outnumbering marinas, boat ramps are used throughout the coastal area for launching into bayous, estuaries, rivers, or directly into the Gulf.

    This map shows marinas and boat launches in the coastal areas of the U.S. Gulf states. The facilities shown do not represent a complete inventory of facilities available. Due to the effects of hurricanes, economic issues, and other factors, facilities may have been damaged, rebuilt, abandoned, or newly developed.
