• Shipping and Navigation

    Shipping fairways are designed to provide safe and efficient access routes for vessels coming in and out of Gulf of Mexico ports. Ten of the top fifteen United States ports, as ranked by total tons, are located in the Gulf of Mexico (US Army Corps of Engineers, 2009).

    As a reflection of the large shipping volume coming into the Gulf, numerous wrecks are found in the coastal zone and into the deeper waters of the Gulf of Mexico. As part of the commerce activities, man-made obstructions and cables are also identified on the map.
