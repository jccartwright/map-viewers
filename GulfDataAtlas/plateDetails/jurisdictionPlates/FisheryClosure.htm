• Fishery Closures
    (Seasonal/Area/Quota Closures)

    These Gulf Exclusive Economic Zone (EEZ) seasonal/area/quota closures are management measures approved by the Gulf of Mexico Fishery Management Council and implemented by NOAA National Marine Fisheries Service for a variety of reasons, including, but not limited to: protecting older and larger fish, protecting critical spawning stock biomass, reducing discards, reducing overfishing, and generally leading to more sustainable fish stocks. The purpose of the management measures is to implement the Fishery Management Plans prepared under the Magnuson-Stevens Act.

    These data represent the Code of Federal Regulations, Title 50: 1) Part 622, Subparts pertaining to the Gulf of Mexico, and 2) for Desoto Canyon; sections 635.2, definitions, and 635.21, Atlantic Highly Migratory Species gear operation and deployment restriction areas. Browse the Electronic Federal Code of Regulations at ecfr.gpoaccess.gov.

    For more detailed information, federal regulations, maps, and links to data downloads for 50 CFR part 622 and 50 CFR section 635.2 (Desoto Canyon), please see 'More Information' section below.
