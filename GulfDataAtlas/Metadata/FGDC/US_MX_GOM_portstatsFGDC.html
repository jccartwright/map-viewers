Description:
Abstract:
This GIS dataset (US_MX_GOM_portstats.shp) contains annual trade statistics from 2005 to 2012 for US and MX ports in the Gulf of Mexico. Tonnage summaries for US ports include imports, exports, total foreign trade, total domestic trade, and total trade (foreign and domestic). Summaries for MX ports also include entries and outputs for domestic trade.
Purpose:
To depict trends in trade and port traffic in the Gulf of Mexico.

Process_Step:
Process_Description:
The NGA WPI shapefile was added into ArcMap 10.2. The attribute table was opened and the Mexico ports in the Gulf of Mexico were selected. The selected data were exported from the attribute table as a .dbf. The .dbf was then opened in Microsoft Excel and all data columns except for the port name and latitude and longitude were deleted. The port statistics for the Mexico ports (foreign imports, foreign exports, total foreign trade, domestic entries, domestic outputs, total domestic trade and total foreign and domestic trade, in metric tonnes) were added to the spreadsheet in separate columns for each statistic type and year in the rows corresponding to the same ports. The US statistics were first converted using Micorsoft Excel from short tons to metric tonnes, equivalent to the MX statistics. The US port names, coordinates, and statistics from each principle port Excel spreadsheet for the years 2005-2012 were then cut and pasted into the spreadsheet containing the Mexico data in the appropriate columns. A shapefile was then created from the resulting spreadsheet in ArcMap 10.2 by right-clicking on the file name in the Catalog window and dselecting "Create feature class... from xy table".

Entity_and_Attribute_Information:
Overview_Description:
Entity_and_Attribute_Overview:
This dataset contains the following attributes: Port Name: the name of the port, as given by the NGA WPI for the MX ports and by the USACE WCSC for the US ports. Country: the name of the country where the port is located, as given by the NGA WPI for the MX ports and by the USACE WCSC for the US ports. Latitude: the y-coordinate for the port sites. Longitude: the x-coordinate for the port sites. IMP_F_XXX: foreign imports, where XXXX is a year ranging from 2005-2012, in metric tonnes. EXP_F_XXXX: foreign exports, where XXXX is a year ranging from 2005-2012, in metric tonnes. TOT_F_XXXX: total foreign trade (imports + exports), where XXXX is a year ranging from 2005-2012, in metric tonnes. ENT_D_XXXX: domestic trade entries, where XXXX is a year ranging from 2005-2012, in metric tonnes. OUT_D_XXXX: domestic trade outputs, where XXXX is a year ranging from 2005-2012, in metric tonnes. TOT_D_XXXX: total domestic trade (entries + outputs), where XXXX is a year ranging from 2005-2012, in metric tonnes. TOT_XXXX: total trade (total foreign + total domestic), where XXXX is a year ranging from 2005-2012, in metric tonnes.
