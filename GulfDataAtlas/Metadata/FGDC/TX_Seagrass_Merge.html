Description:
Abstract:
Seagrass layer created by combining data from Texas Parks and Wildlife, US Fish and Wildlife, Texas A&M, and Texas General Land Ofice. Cartographic analysis and display.
Purpose:
To show the location and extent of seagrass beds in the coastal areas of Texas.
