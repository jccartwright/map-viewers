Title:
USGS Gulfwide SAV 1940-2003
Geospatial_Data_Presentation_Form:
vector digital data
Description:
Abstract:
This dataset is a compilation of submerged aquatic vegetation (SAV) GIS maps developed by the U.S. Geological Survey using a various surveying and image analysis techniques. This data set includes information from 1940 to 2003.
Purpose:
To provide a single shapefile showing the extent of USGS SAV mapping in the Gulf of Mexico from 1940 to 2003.
