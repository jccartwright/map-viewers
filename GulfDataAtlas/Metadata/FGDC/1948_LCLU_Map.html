Description:
Abstract:
Land cover land use (LCLU) geographic information system (GIS) data for 1948 for watersheds in Mobile and Baldwin Counties was derived from a digitized paper map. The LCLU dataset includes four land cover land use classes: Urban, Timber, Crop or Pasture, and Crop.
Purpose:
The 1948 LCLU data was used as input to a hydrologic modeling system to determine the effects of LCLU change on shallow water aquatic ecosystems for watersheds contiguous to the Mobile Bay estuary under various historical scenarios. The 1948 LCLU data was used as the pre-development scenario in the model.
Supplemental_Information:
The 1948 paper map was digitized in a GIS prior to being used as an input for hydrologic models. The digital map is made available in the Gulf of Mexico Digital Atlas (see Online Linkage).

Overview_Description:
Entity_and_Attribute_Overview:
This digitized 1948 LCLU Map for Mobile and Baldwin Counties (digitized from the State of Alabama Land Capability Map (1948)) contains five data classifications: 0 = no data; 1 = Predominantly Row Crop Land, 2 = Predominantly Row Crop or Pasture Land; 3 = Urban Areas; 4 = Predominantly Timber or Range Pasture or Timber Land Only.
