Description:
Abstract:
This datset was extracted from the World Database on Protected Areas (WDPA) geodatabase, a joint project between the United Nations Environment Programme (UNEP) and the International Union for the Coservation of Nature (IUCN). The GIS database lists each site by name and includes management desginations and status.
Purpose:
This dataset shows the location, area and management designation of protected areas in the Republic of Cuba.

Entity_and_Attribute_Information:
Overview_Description:
Entity_and_Attribute_Overview:
This GIS dataset comprises polygons of the protected areas in the Republic of Cuba. Attributes include the site name, area, type of management (national or international), IUCN category and current status (designated or proposed). For a complete description of these attributes and the WDPA reporting requirements, refer to the documents listed below.
