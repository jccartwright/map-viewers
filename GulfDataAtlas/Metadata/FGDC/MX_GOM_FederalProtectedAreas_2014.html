Description:
Abstract:
This dataset shows the location and area for Mexico's Federal Natural Protected Areas within the Coastal Plain and Gulf of Mexico and Yucatan Peninsula and Mexican Caribbean regions as defined by the decrees expressed in the Official Journal of the Federation (Mexico).
Purpose:
To facilitate spatial analyses of natural resources, habitats and populations in the Gulf of Mexico.
