Description:
Abstract:
Chemosynthetic sites are points showing the approximate location of chemosynthetic communities in the Gulf of Mexico. The locations are extrapolated from coordinates from a variety of reports and surveys.
Purpose:
The point file is used as a reference in the environmental review process at Bureau of Ocean Energy Management, Regulation and Enforcement (BOEMRE) Gulf of Mexico OCS Region-Leasing & Environment Section. In addition the information is used in other mapping products at BOEMRE. This shapefile was created to update points in accordance with NTL2009-G40 geographic locations, to incorporate 3 additional points and remove one duplicate point per instruction from Subject Matter Expert, James Sinclair.
Supplemental_Information:
For more information about Chemosynthetic Communities see BOEMRE Gulf of Mexico Environmental information http://www.gomr.boemre.gov/homepg/regulate/environ/chemo/chemo.html

Data_Quality_Information:
Logical_Consistency_Report:
Data is from a variety of sources, therefore reliability of the location will vary.
Completeness_Report:
This data does not identify all the chemosynthetic communities in the Gulf of Mexico. It identifies communities that have been verified to exist. The data represents a point in the gulf whereas a chemosynthetic community inherently will actually dwell in an area.
