Description:
Abstract:
Gridded and blended sea surface vector winds from multiple satellites with direction from NCEP Reanalysis-2; Global ocean coverage with a 0.25-degree resolution; The whole datasets covers from July 1987 to present, monthly resolution in this dataset; 6-hourly and daily are also available in other directories; See http://www.ncdc.noaa.gov/oa/rsad/blendedseawinds.html and links within for details. Include (u,v) means and scalar mean speed w for comparison.
Purpose:
Please note that these are research products, thus, they are considered as experimental in nature. Users are encouraged to register to receive update notices and to provide comments. Please see this link for information on how to register, http://www.ncdc.noaa.gov/oa/rsad/air-sea/seawinds.html
