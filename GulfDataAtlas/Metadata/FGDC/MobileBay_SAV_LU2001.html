Description:
Abstract:
This dataset forms part of four projections for a Mobile Bay Land Cover Land Use (LCLU) Project to link changes in watershed (development, land use) to changes in nearshore and coastal habitats. This dataset represents areas that are forecasted to receive sufficient light (>= 18% Surface Irradiance) for submerged aquatic vegetation (SAV)growth based on 2001 land cover.
Purpose:
To identify areas of potential change in the distribution of SAVs due to changes in Land Cover-Land Use in order to inform and help prioritize restoration and conservation efforts in Mobile Bay.
