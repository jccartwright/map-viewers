              RITA/BTS quality checks the attributes after the shapefile was downloaded. RITA/BTS quality checks were to

              states associated to the polygon, or their polygons were associated to the wrong state. RITA/BTS found 
              these errors and contributed with building the dataset. RITA/BTS went through the US territories and 
              updated the FIPS code.
