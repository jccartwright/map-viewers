      Abstract: 
          A shapefile of 2250 coral occurrences in the Gulf of Mexico compiled for multivariate analysis in Etnoyer, 
          2009. Data are derived from several different reports and record keeping institutions, including Smithsonian 
          National Museum of Natural History and Minerals Management Service, among others. For more information see 
          Etnoyer, 2009. Diversity and Distribution of Octocorals in the Gulf of Mexico. PhD dissertation, Texas A&M 
          University- Corpus Christi, Corpus Christi, TX. 185 p.
      Purpose: 
          The original purpose of the datset was to determine the diversity and distribution of deep-sea corals in the 
          Gulf of Mexico, the data were later employed to identify benthic communities potentially impacted by the 
          Deepwater Horizon oil spill, April 2010.
