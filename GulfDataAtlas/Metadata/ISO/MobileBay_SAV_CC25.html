      Abstract: 
          This is one of three datasets that represent projected light levels at different timesteps for Mobile Bay 
          based on hydrodynamic modeling for future climate scenarios. This dataset represents projected areas with 
          high light (>= 18% Surface Irradiance) in Mobile Bay, AL for 2025.
      Purpose: 
          To identify areas of potential change in distribution of SAVs due to changes in Total Suspended Solids (TSS) 
          in Mobile Bay, AL estuaries.
