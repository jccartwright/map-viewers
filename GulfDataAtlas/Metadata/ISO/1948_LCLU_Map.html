      Abstract: 
          Land cover land use (LCLU) geographic information system (GIS) data for 1948 for watersheds in Mobile and 
          Baldwin Counties was derived from a digitized paper map. The LCLU dataset includes four land cover land use 
          classes: Urban, Timber, Crop or Pasture, and Crop.
      Purpose: 
          The 1948 LCLU data was used as input to a hydrologic modeling system to determine the effects of LCLU change 
          on shallow water aquatic ecosystems for watersheds contiguous to the Mobile Bay estuary under various 
          historical scenarios. The 1948 LCLU data was used as the pre-development scenario in the model.
      Credit:
