      Abstract: 
          This is a final classification. It is ready for distribution. This data set is the 2010-era classification of 
          U.S. Florida region. This data set utilized full or partial Landsat scenes which were analyzed according to 
          the Coastal Change Analysis Program (C-CAP) protocol to determine land cover.
      Purpose: 
          To improve the understanding of coastal uplands and wetlands, and their linkages with the distribution, 
          abundance, and health of living marine resources.
