      Abstract: 
          Chemosynthetic sites are points showing the approximate location of chemosynthetic communities in the Gulf of 
          Mexico. The locations are extrapolated from coordinates from a variety of reports and surveys.
      Purpose: 
          The point file is used as a reference in the environmental review process at Bureau of Ocean Energy 
          Management, Regulation and Enforcement (BOEMRE) Gulf of Mexico OCS Region-Leasing & Environment Section. In 
          addition the information is used in other mapping products at BOEMRE. This shapefile was created to update 
          points in accordance with NTL2009-G40 geographic locations, to incorporate 3 additional points and remove 
          one duplicate point per instruction from Subject Matter Expert, James Sinclair.
