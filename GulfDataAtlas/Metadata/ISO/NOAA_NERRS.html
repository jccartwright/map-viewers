          This dataset contains the geographic boundaries of five estuarine research reserves found in the Gulf of 
          Mexico: Apalachicola National Estuarine Research Reserve (ANERR), Grand Bay National Estuarine Research 
          Reserve (GBNERR), Mission-Aransas National Estuarine Research Reserve (MANERR), Rookery Bay National 
          Estuarine Research Reserve (RBNERR), and Weeks Bay National Estuarine Research Reserve (WBNERR).
      Purpose: 
          The data provide users with information as to the boundaries and locations of these reserves for use in GIS 
          and other applications.
