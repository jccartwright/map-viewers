      Abstract: 
          This dataset contains track lines from 1851 - 2012 based on data from the International Best Track Archive for 
          Climate Stewardship (IBTrACS) and the National Hurricane Center. Only tracks that entered the Gulf of Mexico 
          are included. For example, storms that moved westward across Florida, but did not emerge into the Gulf, were 
          excluded. Only storms that were at tropical storm strength or greater intensity while in the Gulf of Mexico 
          are included. For example, previously strong storms that were downgraded to tropical depressions before 
          crossing into the Gulf were excluded.
      Purpose: 
          This dataset was developed to include in the Gulf of Mexico Data Atlas to show hurricane frequency.
