      Abstract: 
          Marinas in the coastal counties of Texas are included in this data layer. Marina information was gathered from 
          various sources including Google Maps, www.marinas.com, and the Yellow Pages (www.YP.com). This layer does 
          not include all available marina facilities. Due to hurricanes in recent years affecting the Texas coast, 
          some marinas may no longer be available.
      Purpose: 
          This dataset was developed to include in the Gulf of Mexico Data Atlas to show recreational facilities.

              Description: 
                  A search was done with Google Earth for marinas in the coastal Texas area. Names, addresses, and 
                  locations for the facilities were compiled in an Excel spreadsheet. Google Earth was used to 
                  determine coordinates. Addresses were confirmed using other websites including Google Maps, 
                  www.marinas.com, www.yp.com (the Yellow Pages) and the marina's own website, if available. During 
                  the course of confirming addresses, other marinas were discovered and added. The spreadsheet was 
                  converted to a shapefile and uploaded into ArcSDE.
