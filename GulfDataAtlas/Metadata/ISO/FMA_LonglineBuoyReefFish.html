      Abstract: 
          These data represent the 50 CFR Part 622.34, Gulf EEZ seasonal and/or area closures as designated by Federal 
          Register Vol. 61, No. 34934, July 3, 1996. Rules and regulations as amended through 70 FR 76219, December 
          23, 2005. From http://ecfr.gpoaccess.gov "Reef fish longline and buoy gear restricted area. A person aboard 
          a vessel that uses, on any trip, longline or buoy gear in the longline and buoy gear restricted area is 
          limited on that trip to the bag limits for Gulf reef fish specified in ยง622.39(b)(1) and, for Gulf reef fish 
          for which no bag limit is specified in ยง622.39(b)(1), the vessel is limited to 5 percent, by weight, of all 
          fish on board or landed."
      Purpose: 
          The purpose of these data is to visually represent the seasonal and/or area closures for GIS analysis and 
          display. This does not necessarily reflect the latest information contained in the Code of Federal 
          Regulations. Users must assume responsibility to determine the appropriate use of these data as the currency 
          and/or accuracy of these data cannot be guaranteed.
