Basemap/reference layers:

  • Shaded Relief (GEBCO_2014): A shaded-relief visualization of the GEBCO_2014 Grid. This visualization of the GEBCO_2014 grid was produced by NOAA/NCEI. More information on NOAA's GeoPlatform.
  • Global Multi-Resolution Topography (GMRT) synthesis, Lamont-Doherty Earth Observatory, Columbia University. Two versions of the basemap are available: masked (highlights high-resolution multibeam swaths and contributed DEMs), and unmasked.
  • Shaded Relief (ETOPO1): A visualization of the ETOPO1 Global Relief Model. More information on NOAA's GeoPlatform.
  • Light Gray: Esri Light Gray Canvas
  • Arctic Basemap: A visualization of the IBCAO and GEBCO_08 grids. More information on NOAA's GeoPlatform.
  • Antarctic Basemap: A visualization of the IBCSO and GEBCO_08 grids. More information on NOAA's GeoPlatform.
